Ship Design & Engineering

Bay-Tech has developed strengths in conceptual, basic and detailed ship design. Our focus is to help achieve the right balance in terms of design and operational capability for the vessel. We have also developed the necessary skills for design in all kinds of cargo ships, passenger ship, tug boat, patrol boat, self-unloading stone carrier and many more. We possess in-house competence for core activities such as:

Conceptual design.
Basic / initial design.
Production / detailed design
Design modification.
Vessel hull modeling.
Vessel structural analysis.
