About Bay-Tech Group

“Professional Ship Designer and Ship Builder” for public & private clients with finances saving economy.

BAY-TECH provides ship design, Shipbuilding, Repair works, Non-destructive testing (NDT), Shipbuilding consultancy, and logistics services to the marine industry. Since its inception in 2007, Bay-Tech has built a strong reputation for reliable and quality products by designing, shipbuilding and consulting on more than 300 different types of vessels.

Quality Shipbuilding is our motto.

Engr. Md. Tazul Islam, Chairman

Mission Statement

Our primary mission is to design and build custom vessels as per client’s requirement according to classification societies standards for both the inland and seagoing markets on a foundation of quality. The shipyard will be an independent and autonomous partner for their customers and other interested parties. We will strive to provide maximum added value by focusing on the latest technology (both in design and production).

Modern ship design & Quality ship building.

Bay-Tech shipbuilders was instituted in 2016 on the bank of the river old Brahmaputra near Langalbandh, Narayanganj by visionary naval architect Md. Tazul Islam for blending quality shipbuilding with innovative technology at an affordable price. The company enjoys a unique position in Bangladesh where it has built most of its commercial ships. Production area comprises around 17,000 square meters with modern types of machinery and equipment.
